Privacy Policy

ProgressBar · Last updated: 24 March 2026

This privacy policy describes ProgressBar (the “App”): a personal goal and progress tracker that runs on your device. It explains what information is involved when you use the App and related optional features.

Goal data you enter (including titles, progress, check-ins, and notes) is stored on your device. You can use the App without signing in with Apple. Importing and exporting JSON backup files does not require Apple Sign In.

If you choose Sign in with Apple, Apple may share account identifiers with the App according to your Apple settings and Apple’s terms, so progress can be associated with your Apple account for continuity (for example, flows involving iCloud). You can manage or revoke access from your Apple ID settings.

If you use iCloud, encrypted snapshots may sync through Apple’s iCloud services, subject to Apple’s policies and your device settings. You can disable or limit iCloud for the App in system settings.

Exported JSON files are saved to locations you choose (for example Files or iCloud Drive). You control those files after export. Imported files are processed on-device to restore your goals.

Depending on where you live, you may have statutory privacy rights (access, correction, deletion, etc.). For questions about this App, use the support options on the App Store product page. We may update this policy; the latest text will appear in the App.

隐私政策（简体中文）

以下为 ProgressBar（「本应用」）的摘要说明。更新日期：2026 年 3 月 24 日。

本隐私政策说明本应用如何处理信息。本应用用于在设备上记录个人目标与进度，并说明使用本应用及可选功能时涉及的数据。

你在本应用中输入的目标数据（含标题、进度、打卡与备注等）主要保存在本机。你可以在不使用「通过 Apple 登录」的情况下使用本应用。通过 JSON 进行导入或导出备份，并不依赖 Apple 登录。

若你选择「通过 Apple 登录」，Apple 可能依据你的 Apple 设置与 Apple 条款向本应用提供账户标识符，以便在涉及例如 iCloud 的流程中将进度与你的 Apple 账户关联。你可以在 Apple ID 设置中管理或撤销相关授权。

若你使用 iCloud，加密快照可能通过 Apple 的 iCloud 服务同步，并受 Apple 政策及你设备上的设置约束。你可以在系统设置中关闭或限制本应用的 iCloud。

导出的 JSON 文件将保存到你选择的位置（例如「文件」或 iCloud 云盘）。导出后文件由你自行保管与分享；导入的文件仅在本机处理以恢复你的目标数据。

根据你所在地区，你可能依法享有查阅、更正、删除等权利。若对本应用有疑问，请通过 App Store 产品页所列的支持渠道联系。我们可能更新本政策，最新版本将显示在本应用内。
